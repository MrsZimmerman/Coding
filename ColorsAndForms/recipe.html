Mrs. Zimmerman's Recipe

Salsa Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 - 2 Frozen Chicken Breasts
  • 1 can of Salsa
  • 1 bag of frozen corn
  • 1 can of black beans - drained
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 container of sour cream
  • 1 bag of tortilla chips

Instructions

  1. Open jar of salsa and pour 1/3 of the jar in the bottom of a crockpot
  2. Place the frozen chicken breast on top of the salsa
  3. Open and pour of bag of frozen corn on top of the chicken
  4. Open, drain, and pour the can of black beans on top of the chicken
  5. Pour the remaining 2/3 of the jar of salsa on top of the chicken
  6. Mix the salsa, corn, and black beans together, being sure to cover the chicken
  7. Cook in the crockpot on LOW for 8 hours
  8. When fully cooked, shred the chicken, and stir together
  9. Serve in a bowl with cheese, sour cream and tortilla chips
