Interesting things about the internet:

  1. The two people instrumental in the development of the internet were Bob Kahn and Vint Cerf.
  2. The internet was first developed to allow for military communication.
  3. Noone (but also everyone) is in charge of the internet.
  4. The internet is made up of distributed packet sending network of computers.
  5. The players of the internet are motivated to make sure it works, because it is to their benefit.
  6. They want to be able to communicate smoothly and easily.

Interesting Facts about the World Wide Web

  • The inventor of the World Wide Web is Sir Tim Berners-Lee who was born and lived in England.
  • Berners-Lee attended Oxford University.
  • The idea of the web came to him while he was working at CERN, the European Organization for Nuclear Research. They operate the largest partical physics lab in the world.
  • The work and the value of what Berners-Lee has done led him to be knighted by Queen Elizabeth in 2004.
  • One of the most interesting facts about Berners-Lee is that he has never profitted directly by his work on developing the web. He has chosen to put the benefits for others above his own finanical gain.
