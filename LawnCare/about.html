About Us

Zimmermans Landscaping was started in 1983. Our founder, James, received a horticulture degree from Delaware Valley College in 1982. GO AGGIES!! Since then, we have strived to create a culture of excellence as we work hard to make you (and your landscaping) look good. It is our motto to do everything with the utmost honesty and integrity, and to ensure that the customer is happy.

Whatever your landscaping needs from patios, decks, and walkways to trees, shrubs, and flowers you are sure to be impressed with the our knowledge and know how.
